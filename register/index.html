Cancellation / transfer policies

Registrations for CANHEIT 2013 may be cancelled or transferred, according to the policies outlined below.

CANCELLATION POLICY: Cancellations must be sent by email to canheit@uOttawa.ca by May 10, 2013. A $75 administrative fee will apply to all cancellation requests; refunds will be issued after the conference. No refunds will be issued for cancellations received after May 10, 2013 or for “No-Show” registrants.

TRANSFER POLICY: If you are unable to attend for any reason, you may transfer your registration to another individual for a fee of $75. The registrant must provide a written request and a complete registration form for the replacement person by May 10, 2013. Please send the request by email to canheit@uOttawa.ca.
