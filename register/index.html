Cancellation / transfer policies

Registrations for CANHEIT 2013 may be cancelled or transferred, according to the policies outlined below.

Cancellation policy

Cancellations must be sent by email to canheit@uOttawa.ca by May 10, 2013. A $75 administrative fee will apply to all cancellation requests; refunds will be issued after the conference. No refunds will be issued for cancellations received after May 10, 2013 or for “No-Show” registrants.

Virtual attendance refunds: We're all fans of new and cool technology but we understand that sometimes it doesn't always work as expected. If you've chosen the "Virtual Attendance" option, you'll be offered a trial period to test the technology, and we'll offer you a full refund until May 31, 2013 if you just can't get the virtual environment working on your system. No refunds will be issued for cancellations received after May 31, 2013 or for “No-Show” registrants.

Transfer policy

If you are unable to attend for any reason, you may transfer your registration to another individual for a fee of $75. The registrant must provide a written request and a complete registration form for the replacement person by May 10, 2013. Please send the request by email to canheit@uOttawa.ca.

Virtual attendance transfers: We know that you'd love to experience our 3D CANHEIT world, but if something comes up and you're unable to participate, you can transfer your virtual registration up until May 31, 2013.

Recording Consent

Photographs and film footage may be taken during this event, which may or may not include your recognizable image or a video. By participating in this event, you consent to being photographed or filmed and authorize the CANHEIT organizers to use the photographs or film in print, digital, video or web-based format for its promotional and archival purposes. If you have any questions regarding this process, please contact us as soon as possible.
