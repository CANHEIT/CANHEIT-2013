About the University of Ottawa:

The University of Ottawa is Canada's premier bilingual university, serving over 40,000 students in the language of their choice. With an academic and support staff numbering over 8,000, the University offers more than 360 undergraduate programs and nearly 120 graduate programs.

The University of Ottawa ranks seventh in research intensity among Canadian Universities, and features the fourth largest co-operative education program in Canada.

Visit the University of Ottawa

About Carleton University:

Carleton University, Canada's Capital University, is home to more than 26,000 graduate and undergraduate students. Founded in 1942 and located on the banks of the Rideau Canal, Carleton University offers 65 programs of study in a variety of fields.

The University employs more than 2,500 faculty and support staff, and it currently has in excess of 850 research projects underway in all five of its academic faculties.
