What is CANHEIT?

CANHEIT is Canada's premier Higher Education IT Conference taking place June 9­-12 in our nation's beautiful capital, Ottawa, Ontario. CANHEIT 2013, and our theme, is all about Exceptional Ideas for a changing world.

Who is it for?

CANHEIT brings together people from all walks of higher ed IT. Whether you are an IT Director looking to gain some strategic insights, a Systems Admin looking for tips to better implement or manage your technology, or a Project Manager looking for new and innovative ways to stay on track, CANHEIT 2013 is the conference for you.

What to expect?

CANHEIT 2013 is going to be a truly exceptional conference. The conference organizing committee has been planning for over a year to make this conference like no other. CANHEIT 2013 will feature:

  • amazing food featuring local flavours (ever tried poutine?)
  • awesome location steps from Parliament Hill and the city’s vibrant core
  • creative and fun events throughout the day providing opportunities to learn, discuss and network
  • fun evening events and entertainment (we hope you fancy a few games and a wee bit of comedy!)
  • internationally acclaimed keynote speakers to inspire your exceptional ideas
  • engaging panel discussions and presentations covering all of the hot topics and challenges facing higher ed IT professionals today
  • and much more!

Expect to leave CANHEIT 2013 with:

Exceptional ideas

There will be over 60 sessions devoted to topics that reflect the IT challenges you face at your institution. Learn from others who have adopted IT solutions and offer insights into the processes and best practices and take away peer experiences and lessons learned.

New and improved technical skills

CANHEIT 2013 will offer hands-on workshops and training; from SANS, to Apple iOS and Mac OS, to Xerox Six Sigma and IBM Virtual Computing Labs, we’re excited to provide opportunities to learn new skills and methodologies that you can take back to the office to positively impact the way you work.

Valuable connections

Connect with other IT professionals and vendors from across Canada during hands-on sessions, networking breaks and social activities. CANHEIT 2013 offers a unique experience to engage and collaborate with peers who experience the same IT challenges you may face at your institution.
