What is CANHEIT?

CANHEIT is Canada's premier Higher Education IT Conference taking place June 9­-12 in our nation's beautiful capital, Ottawa, Ontario. CANHEIT 2013 is all about Exceptional Ideas for a changing world. To reinforce our conference theme, this year’s organizing committee has come up with some exceptional ideas to make this CANHEIT experience different from any other.

Who is it for?

CANHEIT brings together staff, managers, and senior administrators who are responsible for the management and evolution of their campus information systems, learning systems, and technology infrastructure.

Whether you are an IT Director looking to gain some strategic insights, a Systems Admin looking for tips to better implement or manage your technology, or a Project Manager looking for new and innovative ways to stay on track, CANHEIT 2013 is the conference for you.

What to expect?

CANHEIT 2013 is going to be a truly exceptional conference. The conference organizing committee from the University of Ottawa and Carleton University has been planning for over a year to make this conference like no other. CANHEIT 2013 will feature:

  • amazing food featuring local flavours (ever tried poutine?)
  • awesome location steps from Parliament Hill and in a city home to 90% of Canada’s telecommunications research and over 1,800 advanced technology companies
  • creative and fun events throughout the day providing opportunities to learn, discuss and network
  • fun evening events and entertainment entertainment (not only are we planning to dazzle you with the sights and sounds of the Casino du Lac-Leamy, we have it on really good authority that Rick Mercer is going to stop by and spend time with us during the Conference!)
  • internationally acclaimed keynote speakers to inspire your exceptional ideas
  • engaging panel discussions and presentations covering all of the hot topics and challenges facing higher ed IT professionals today
  • and much more!

Expect to leave CANHEIT 2013 with:

Exceptional ideas

There will be over 60 sessions devoted to topics that reflect the IT challenges you face at your institution. Learn from others who have adopted IT solutions and offer insights into the processes and best practices and take away peer experiences and lessons learned; in addition, conference materials such as presentations will be made available to conference delegates. We’ll also have speakers that provide unexpected views about technology and the way it can be used.

New and improved technical skills

CANHEIT 2013 will offer hands-on workshops and training; from SANS, to Apple, to Six Sigma, we’re excited to provide opportunities to learn new skills and methodologies that you can take back to the office to positively impact the way you work.

Valuable connections

Connect with other IT professionals from across Canada during hands-on sessions, networking breaks and social activities. Meet with top IT service providers that are experts in providing solutions and applying best practices to technological challenges. CANHEIT 2013 offers a unique experience to engage and collaborate with peers who experience the same IT challenges you may face at your institution.
