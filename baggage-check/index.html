Baggage Check

Wednesday morning, leave your luggage at the Registration desk at FSS.

Sponsored by Avaya

Steps

  1. Drop-off your luggage at the Registration desk at FSS.
  2. Fill-out a luggage tag, and we'll give you a claim tag.
  3. Pick-up your luggage using your claim tag. The registration desk is manned all day Wednesday.
