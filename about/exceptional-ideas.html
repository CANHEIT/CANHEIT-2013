When Craig Buchanan, Manager – User Services, Concordia University said “Let me attend from my office (I supply my own coffee J).” we thought, what an EXCEPTIONAL idea!

We made it so that Craig can do this. And you can too! For the first time ever, CANHEIT will be offering virtual attendance to keynotes and sessions! Find out more .

Guidebook App

“Take advantage of the GuideBook App (guidebook.com).” said Andrew Tomkins, Systems Administrator, University of Toronto, Mississauga. “When we hosted OUCC at UTM (yay!), we had excellent feedback on the online scheduling. Mike Young, Systems Analyst, University of Toronto, Mississauga agreed, “Use the Guidebook App! It worked well for OUCC.”

This was an awesome idea Andrew and Mike! Take advantage, we did! If you haven’t already seen it yet, the Guidebook is already in action. You can download the CANHEIT 2013 app on your iOS device or Android device today!
