Checklist for your proposal

Evaluation of proposals will be based on crowd-voting (come back for that in February and through an Evaluation committee process).

Don't sabotage your proposal! Make sure you include the following:

  • connection to the theme "Exceptional ideas for a changing world"
  • promotes IT in higher-ed (particularly in Canada)
  • potential to engage and excite delegates
  • an abstract that is 250-500 words long
